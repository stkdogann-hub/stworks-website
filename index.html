About Us

THE STUDIO

STworks Design & Creative LLC is a South Florida-based mural art and design studio. We specialize in transforming ordinary walls into extraordinary works of art.

From luxury Airbnbs in Palm Beach to buzzing restaurants in West Palm Beach, our murals don't just decorate — they define spaces. Every project starts with a conversation and ends with a wall that stops people in their tracks.

We bring together artistic vision, technical precision, and a deep understanding of how spaces work. Whether it's a 10-foot accent wall or a 50-foot building exterior, we deliver work that's bold, beautiful, and built to last.

Common Questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know about commissioning a custom mural.

Mural pricing depends on size, complexity, and style. Simple abstract murals start around $15 per square foot, while detailed realistic work can range from $25-$40+ per square foot. Use our Quote Calculator above for an instant estimate, or contact us for a precise quote tailored to your project.

Timeline varies by project size. A small accent wall (under 100 sq ft) typically takes 2-3 days. Medium murals (100-300 sq ft) take about 1-2 weeks. Large-scale exterior murals (500+ sq ft) can take 2-4 weeks. Design and concept phase adds 1-2 weeks before painting begins.

We serve all of South Florida including West Palm Beach, Palm Beach, Jupiter, Boca Raton, Delray Beach, Fort Lauderdale, and Miami. We also take on special projects throughout the state and occasionally nationally.

Absolutely! Every project includes digital mockups and design renderings so you can see exactly what the finished mural will look like on your wall. We offer revisions until you're 100% satisfied with the design before we start painting.

With proper wall preparation and UV-protective sealant, outdoor murals in South Florida typically last 5-10+ years. We use high-quality exterior-grade paints and apply multiple coats of anti-UV sealer to protect against Florida's intense sun and rain.

We require a 30% deposit to begin the design phase, 40% before painting starts, and the remaining 30% upon completion. We accept bank transfers, checks, and major credit cards. All projects include a detailed contract for your protection.
